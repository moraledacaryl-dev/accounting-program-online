# Pass 5 — People, Attendance, Payroll, Users, and Permissions

This pass establishes a coherent People & Payroll operating area across employee records, attendance, payroll periods, approvals, payroll integration, user accounts, roles, and the staff guide.

Implemented:
- persistent People & Payroll context navigation;
- direct movement between employee, attendance, payroll, approval, user, and access-control workflows;
- responsive horizontally scrollable navigation on mobile;
- stronger sticky table headers and numeric scanning for employee, attendance, and payroll records;
- improved scroll targeting for long roles-permissions and staff-guide pages;
- preservation of all existing APIs, payroll calculations, attendance imports, permissions, and approval behavior.

The navigation intentionally distinguishes employee identity from user-account access and payroll processing from managerial approval.
