# Pass 6 — Inventory, Procurement, Receiving, and Restaurant Operations

This pass connects inventory, purchasing, receiving, and food operations into one predictable operational area.

## Implemented

- Persistent Inventory & Operations navigation across inventory, stock movements, reconciliation, suppliers, purchase requests, purchase orders, receiving, restaurant operations, menu/recipes, and staff meals.
- Direct actions for creating a purchase request and receiving stock.
- Clear active-section treatment and horizontally scrollable mobile navigation.
- Sticky operational table headers and stronger scan hierarchy for stock, procurement, receiving, and restaurant records.
- Safer mobile table containment and improved form scroll targeting.
- Existing inventory calculations, procurement approvals, receiving behavior, recipes, staff-meal posting, and integrations remain unchanged.

## Workflow ownership

Inventory Items remains the catalogue and stock-control starting point. Purchase Requests initiate demand, Purchase Orders authorize supplier purchasing, Receiving confirms delivered quantities and costs, and Restaurant Operations consumes approved menu and recipe data.
